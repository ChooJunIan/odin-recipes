Fried Rice

Description

This is the vege fried rice recipe that is simple to replicate and prepare for anytime, anyday!

Ingredients

  • Rice (leftover rice is even better!)
  • Spring Onions
  • Carrot
  • Red bell pepper
  • French beans
  • Salt, garlic, ginger
  • Sugar, black pepper
  • Oil

Steps to Prepare

  1. Heating the Oil and Cook the Spring Onions + Vegetables

    2. Heat oil in a pan or wok on medium-high heat. Add ginger and garlic and saute for a few seconds.

    Stir in the spring onions and saute for 30 seconds.

    Add in all the veggies, and stir fry for 2-3 minutes until all the veggies are tender and crisp.

  2. Add the Sauces

    3. Stir in the soy sauce, green chili sauce, vinegar, salt, pepper, and sugar. Mix them well.

  3. Now for the main ingredient: RICE

    4. Now add the cooked rice and gently toss for 2-3 minutes until it is well combined. Taste and test. Add and adjust sauces to your own tastes and preferences.

    Finally, garnish with spring onions and greens.

Vege Fried Rice is now ready to be served!
